Barley Porridge

  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup honey
  1. In a large saucepan, combine the pearl barley, water, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 45-60 minutes.
  2. Stir in the butter and cream, and continue to cook for 5-10 minutes, or until the porridge reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Serve hot with honey.
